Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a consultation?
Use our Contact page or WhatsApp +91 95653 21625. We’ll share available slots and what to bring.
Braces vs. clear aligners—what’s right for me?
Braces are fixed and versatile for all severities. Aligners are removable and discreet—great for mild–moderate cases. The best option is recommended after an exam and bite analysis.
How long does treatment take?
Most treatments take 6–24 months depending on complexity and compliance. You’ll receive a personalized timeline at consultation.
Is orthodontic treatment painful?
Mild soreness for 1–3 days after activations or new aligners is common and settles quickly. Our comfort protocol helps a lot.
Do you treat international patients?
Absolutely. We coordinate documentation, scheduling, and remote follow-ups. See International Patients for the full journey.

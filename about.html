Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a consultation?
Use our Contact page or WhatsApp +91 9565321625. We’ll share available slots and what to bring for your first visit.
Braces vs. clear aligners—what’s right for me?
Braces are fixed and versatile for all severities. Aligners are removable and discreet, great for mild–moderate cases. Dr. Singh recommends the best option after your exam and bite analysis.
How long does treatment take?
Most treatments take 6–24 months depending on complexity and compliance. You’ll receive a personalized timeline at your consultation.
Is orthodontic treatment painful?
You may feel pressure/soreness for a few days after activations or new aligners. It settles quickly—our comfort protocol and tips help a lot.
Do you treat international patients?
Absolutely. We coordinate documentation, scheduling, and remote follow-ups. See the International Patients page for the full journey.

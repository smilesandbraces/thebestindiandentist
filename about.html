About Dr. Vikramjeet Singh

At Smiles & Braces Dental Clinic, we believe every smile tells a story. Dr. Vikramjeet Singh (BDS, MDS & Advanced Orthodontic Training) has devoted over 15 years to perfecting those stories. His passion for dentistry and commitment to continuing education ensure that you receive the most advanced treatments in a caring environment.

Our Journey

  • 2005 – Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS)

    Graduated with honours from a premier dental college, laying a strong foundation in clinical dentistry.

  • 2008 – Master of Dental Surgery (MDS)

    Completed specialised training in orthodontics, mastering the art and science of aligning teeth and jaws.

  • 2010 – Advanced Orthodontic Training

    Undertook advanced orthodontic courses internationally to stay abreast of cutting‑edge techniques and technologies.

  • 2010–Present – Transforming Smiles

    Founded Smiles & Braces Dental Clinic in Lucknow, where thousands of patients have regained confidence through personalised dental care.

Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence in Dental Care

Founded in 2008 by Dr. Vikram Jeet Singh, Smile & Braces Dental Clinic has been at the forefront of dental innovation in Lucknow, providing world-class care to patients from across India and around the globe.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to make exceptional dental care accessible to everyone. Over the years, we have grown from a small practice to a premier dental clinic, serving thousands of patients and earning recognition as one of the leading dental facilities in North India.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to combining cutting-edge technology with personalized, compassionate care. Every member of our team shares the same dedication to excellence, ensuring that each patient receives the highest standard of treatment in a comfortable, welcoming environment.

Meet Our Chief Dental Officer

Dr. Vikram Jeet Singh

Dr. Vikram Jeet Singh is a highly accomplished dental surgeon with over 15 years of experience in comprehensive dental care, specializing in cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, and dental implantology.

After completing his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) from King George's Medical University, Lucknow, Dr. Singh pursued his Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) with a specialization in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery. His commitment to continuous learning has led him to complete advanced training programs in implantology and cosmetic dentistry from renowned institutions in India and abroad.

Dr. Singh's philosophy centers on providing personalized, patient-centered care that combines the latest technological advances with time-tested dental principles. His gentle approach and attention to detail have earned him the trust of thousands of patients, including many international visitors who travel specifically to receive his expert care.

Advanced Technology

State-of-the-Art Equipment for Superior Care

We invest in the latest dental technology to ensure precise diagnoses, comfortable treatments, and exceptional results for our patients.

Advanced digital radiography with 90% less radiation exposure and instant, high-resolution images for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.

  • Cone Beam CT (CBCT) Technology
  • Panoramic Digital X-rays

About Dr Vikramjeet Singh

Dr Vikramjeet Singh is a qualified orthodontist (BDS, MDS & Advanced Orthodontic Training) with over 15 years of dedicated service in orthodontics and general dentistry. He founded Smiles & Braces Dental Clinic in Lucknow to provide world-class dental care, specializing in braces, aligners, and dental implants. His commitment to continuing education ensures patients receive the latest in dental technology and treatment protocols.

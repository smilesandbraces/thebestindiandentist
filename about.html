Braces vs aligners—what’s right for me?
Braces are fixed and versatile. Aligners are removable and discreet—great for mild–moderate cases. The best option is recommended after an exam.

How long does treatment take?
Most plans take 6–24 months depending on complexity and cooperation. You’ll receive a personalized timeline at consultation.

Braces vs. clear aligners—what’s right for me?
Both effectively move teeth. Braces are fixed and versatile; aligners are removable and discreet. Dr. Singh recommends options after examining your bite, spacing, and lifestyle.
How long does treatment take?
Most orthodontic cases range from 6–24 months depending on complexity and compliance. You’ll receive a personalized timeline after assessment.
Is orthodontic treatment painful?
You may feel mild pressure or tenderness for a few days after adjustments or new aligners. This is normal and settles quickly; comfort tips are shared at each visit.
Do you treat international patients?
Yes. We help coordinate schedules, documentation, and follow-ups. See our International Patients page for the step-by-step journey.

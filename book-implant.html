Dental Implant FAQs

How long does a dental implant take?

Placement 30–60 mins; healing 3–4 months; then the crown. Immediate loading may be possible in selected cases.

Is the procedure painful?

Local anesthesia and gentle technique make it comfortable. Mild soreness for 24–48 hours is common and manageable.

What is the cost range?

Costs vary by system, bone condition, and crown type. We provide a written plan with itemized pricing and EMI options after assessment.

Am I a candidate for implants?

Most adults are candidates. We evaluate bone volume, gums, bite, and medical history to decide the best plan for you.

Do you offer EMI?

Yes. We can guide you through partner-financing options after your treatment plan is finalized.

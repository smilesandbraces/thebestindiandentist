Meet Dr. Vikram Jeet Singh

Dr. Vikram Jeet Singh

Dr. Vikram Jeet Singh is a highly qualified orthodontist with over 15 years of experience delivering exceptional dental care. Holding a BDS and MDS along with advanced orthodontic training, he is committed to patient comfort, clinical excellence, and aesthetic precision.

Since establishing Smiles & Braces Dental Clinic in Lucknow, Dr. Singh has become a trusted name for both local and international patients seeking:

  • State-of-the-art orthodontic treatments
  • Comprehensive dental alignments and braces
  • Customized treatment plans with a focus on comfort
  • Continuing care and follow-up support

His passion for continuing education ensures the clinic always offers the latest techniques and technologies. International patients benefit from his seamless coordination of travel, accommodation recommendations, and post-treatment care — making every visit stress-free and comfortable.

Say Goodbye to Painful Root Canals

Root Canal Treatment no longer has to be a nightmare. At Smiles & Braces Dental Clinic, Lucknow, Dr Vikram Jeet Singh offers laser-assisted painless RCT that removes infection with zero discomfort, zero vibration, and faster healing.

Why Choose Laser RCT?

  • Completely Painless: No drilling vibration or post-treatment ache.
  • High-precision lasers sterilize deep canals, reducing reinfection risk.
  • Single-Sitting RCT: Most cases completed in one visit.
  • Better healing and reduced swelling due to minimal tissue trauma.
  • Perfect for patients with dental anxiety or low pain tolerance.

Technology That Makes a Difference

We use advanced German diode lasers and apex-locator systems ensuring 100 % accuracy. Every procedure is performed under magnification for superior precision and patient comfort.

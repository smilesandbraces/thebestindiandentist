Why see an orthodontist (not just any dentist)?

Orthodontists complete additional, focused training to correct tooth alignment, jaw relationships, and bite function. That specialization matters for predictable, stable results — especially for complex cases, teen growth guidance, relapse after previous treatment, or adult aesthetic plans that must protect enamel and gums.

  • Accurate diagnosis and biomechanics for efficient tooth movement
  • Long-term stability through individualized retention protocols
  • Conservative plan sequencing to protect teeth and restorations

Clear aligners vs braces: which is better for you?

Both work — the right choice depends on your bite, lifestyle, compliance, and budget. We plan aligners and braces with digital records, simulations, and measured checkpoints. If aligners are chosen, you’ll receive numbered trays, wear-time guidance, and hygiene instructions. If braces are preferred, we’ll discuss bracket type, esthetics (ceramic options), and expected timelines.

Tip: Aligners are fantastic for aesthetics and hygiene. Braces can provide more direct control in certain complex movements. We’ll tell you honestly what fits your case.

Your orthodontic journey with us (step-by-step)

  1. Consult & Scans: Photos, impressions/scans, and bite analysis.
  2. Diagnosis & Plan: We present options with pros/cons, cost, and timeline.
  3. Start of Treatment: Attachment of braces or delivery of your aligner kit.

FAQs: Orthodontist near Munshi Pulia

How long will my treatment take?
Most cases finish between 8–18 months depending on complexity and compliance. We’ll give you a realistic timeline upfront.

Is treatment painful?
Expect mild, short-lived discomfort after adjustments or new trays. We use comfort-focused mechanics and share tips to keep you at ease.

Can I switch from braces to aligners later?
Sometimes yes, depending on progress and goals. We’ll advise on feasibility and costs if you want to switch.

How often are reviews?
Braces: ~4–8 weeks. Aligners: ~6–10 weeks or remote check-ins if suitable.

Our Services

Comprehensive Dental Care

From orthodontic treatments to general dentistry, we provide complete oral health care with modern techniques and personalized attention.

Orthodontic Treatments

Traditional Metal Braces

Reliable and effective orthodontic treatment using high-quality metal brackets and wires for optimal results.

  • Most cost-effective option
  • Suitable for all age groups
  • Highly effective for complex cases
  • Durable and reliable
Book Appointment

Ceramic Braces

Tooth-colored braces that blend naturally with your teeth for a more aesthetic appearance during treatment.

  • Less visible than metal braces
  • Same effectiveness as metal braces
  • Comfortable to wear
  • Stain-resistant materials
Book Appointment

Clear Aligners

Removable, transparent aligners that gradually move teeth into proper position with maximum comfort.

  • Nearly invisible treatment
  • Removable for eating and cleaning
  • Comfortable and smooth
  • Fewer office visits required
Book Appointment

Retainers

Custom-made devices to maintain teeth position after orthodontic treatment for long-lasting results.

  • Prevents teeth from shifting
  • Custom-fitted for comfort
  • Available in removable and fixed options
  • Essential for long-term results
Book Appointment

Self-Ligating Braces

Modern braces that use special clips instead of elastic bands for faster, more comfortable treatment.

  • Fewer clinic visits needed
  • Less discomfort and pressure
  • Easy to maintain oral hygiene
  • Available in metal and ceramic
Book Appointment

Lingual Braces

Invisible braces placed behind the teeth for complete discretion without compromising on effectiveness.

  • Hidden from view
  • Custom-made for each patient
  • Effective for complex cases
  • Ideal for professionals & adults
Book Appointment

Damon Braces

Advanced self-ligating braces designed for faster results with minimal discomfort and fewer adjustments.

  • No need for elastic bands
  • Less friction and pressure
  • Faster treatment in some cases
  • Smooth, rounded brackets
Book Appointment

Interceptive Orthodontics

Early treatment to guide proper jaw growth and prevent future orthodontic problems in children.

  • Designed for children (ages 6-10)
  • Helps guide jaw development
  • Reduces risk of future complications
  • Improves facial symmetry early
Book Appointment

Palatal Expanders

Orthodontic device used to widen the upper jaw, often in children, to improve bite and create more space for teeth.

  • Ideal for growing children
  • Corrects crossbite and overcrowding
  • Non-surgical jaw widening
  • Helps permanent teeth erupt properly
Book Appointment

General Dental Services

Routine Dental Checkups

Comprehensive oral health examinations and preventive care to maintain optimal dental health.

  • Thorough oral examination
  • Professional teeth cleaning
  • Early problem detection
  • Oral health education

Dental Fillings

Restoration of teeth damaged by decay using modern, tooth-colored materials for natural results.

  • Tooth-colored composite fillings
  • Mercury-free options
  • Minimally invasive procedures
  • Natural-looking results

Root Canal Treatment

Advanced endodontic therapy to save infected or damaged teeth with modern pain-free techniques.

  • Pain-free procedures
  • Modern rotary techniques
  • Single-visit treatments available
  • High success rates

Tooth Extractions

Safe and comfortable removal of damaged or problematic teeth with comprehensive post-care.

  • Painless extraction procedures
  • Wisdom tooth removal
  • Surgical extractions available
  • Post-operative care included

Preventive Care

Proactive treatments and education to maintain optimal oral health and prevent dental problems.

  • Fluoride treatments
  • Dental sealants
  • Oral hygiene instruction
  • Diet counseling

Dental Implants

Long-lasting and natural-looking solution to replace missing teeth and restore function and aesthetics.

  • Permanent tooth replacement
  • Natural appearance and feel
  • Restores chewing and speech
  • Preserves jawbone health

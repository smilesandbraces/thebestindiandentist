Why Tartar Removal Matters

Tartar (dental calculus) is hardened plaque that sticks to your teeth and can only be removed by a dental professional. It harbors bacteria that irritate your gums and lead to inflammation and gum disease if left untreated.

By removing tartar regularly, you help prevent gum disease, cavities, and bad breath. Our skilled dentists use advanced tools to gently remove tartar, protecting your dental health and keeping your teeth clean and bright.

Regular tartar removal offers benefits:

  • Prevents gum disease by eliminating bacteria-harboring buildup.
  • Helps avoid cavities and tooth decay that tartar can contribute to.
  • Reduces staining and discoloration for a brighter smile.
  • Improves overall oral health and freshens breath.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is tartar and why remove it?

Tartar is hardened plaque that builds up on teeth. It can only be removed professionally. Removing tartar prevents gum disease, tooth decay, and other oral health issues.

How often should I get a tartar removal?

We recommend a dental cleaning and tartar removal at least every 6 months during your regular checkups. Patients prone to buildup may benefit from more frequent cleanings.

Is tartar removal painful?

No, the procedure is generally not painful. Our trained hygienists use gentle tools to scrape away tartar, and most patients experience little to no discomfort.

Can I remove tartar at home?

No. Attempting to remove tartar yourself can damage your teeth and gums. Professional cleaning is the safe way to remove tartar.

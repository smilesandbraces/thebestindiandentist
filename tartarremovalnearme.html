Why Tartar Removal Matters

Tartar (dental calculus) is hardened plaque that can only be removed by a dental professional. It harbors bacteria that lead to gum inflammation, cavities, and bad breath if left untreated.

  • Prevents gum disease and tooth decay
  • Reduces staining for a whiter smile
  • Improves oral health and breath freshness
  • Protects your gums and enamel

Frequently Asked Questions

What is tartar and why should I remove it?

Tartar is hardened plaque that sticks to teeth. It can only be removed professionally and helps prevent gum disease and decay.

How often should I get a cleaning?

We recommend tartar removal every 6 months. Some patients may need more frequent visits based on their oral condition.

Is it painful?

No, it’s a comfortable procedure performed with modern ultrasonic and hand-scaling tools.

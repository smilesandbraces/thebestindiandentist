Your international patient journey

Simple, safe, time-efficient

  1. Virtual consult

    Share photos/scans, medical history and goals. We discuss options, indicative costs and time windows.

  2. Plan & itinerary

    Written plan with daily chair-time, recovery guidance, and visa letter (if needed).

  3. Fly-in & day-1 review

    Clinical assessment, updated scans, and consent. Airport pickup & hotel coordination available.

  4. Treatment

    Bundled appointments to reduce days in town. Conservative protocols, clear after-care.

  5. Remote follow-ups

    Tele-checks with photo guides; local referrals if anything urgent arises back home.

Typical time windows

  • Smile Refresh: 3–4 days
  • Aligners (start): 5–10 days to first delivery
  • Braces bonding: 1–2 days + periodic virtual reviews
  • Implant stage-1: 3–5 days; stage-2 in ~3–4 months

What to bring

  • Passport & visa copy (if applicable)
  • Medical history & medications
  • Past X-rays/scans or aligner plans (if any)
  • Travel insurance (recommended)

Welcome to Lucknow

Heritage, food and warm people

Lucknow blends historical architecture, refined cuisine and modern conveniences. Safe neighborhoods around Indira Nagar make stays comfortable and accessible.

  • Airport (LKO) → Clinic: ~30–40 min by car
  • Local delicacies: kebabs, sheermal, chaat — many hygienic modern options

  • Weather: pleasant Nov–Feb; pre-monsoon heat Apr–Jun

Frequently asked questions

Can I finish aligner treatment with only two trips?
Often yes. We start with scans & planning, deliver first sets within ~5–10 days, and schedule virtual checks. A second trip finalizes refinements if needed.

Do you help with visa letters?
Yes—we issue appointment confirmation letters with clinic details and itinerary. Please share passport info and tentative dates.

How accurate is the currency conversion?
Rates use a public mid-market feed and auto-refresh. Your bank’s settlement rate and fees may differ slightly.

Is post-treatment care available after I return?
Yes, via scheduled tele-follow ups. For urgent care, we coordinate with a local dentist and share your clinical notes.

Ready to plan your trip?

Share your photos, timeline and goals. We’ll propose the safest, fastest path for your smile.

FAQs

How long do I need to stay in Lucknow?

Most orthodontic starts need 3–5 days. Follow-ups can be clustered with video check-ins in between.

Can I start treatment and continue abroad?

Yes. We coordinate schedules, ship retainers if needed, and arrange teleconsults.

What about pain and downtime?

Mild soreness for 1–3 days after braces activation or new aligners. Most resume sightseeing/work next day.

Do you help with visas and hotels?

Yes, we issue a formal appointment letter for visas and share hotel tie-ups nearby.

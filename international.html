Your International Dental Journey

Trusted worldwide • Safe • Efficient

  1. Virtual Consultation

    Share scans and history online. Dr. Vikram Jeet Singh (MDS) reviews your case, outlines options and provides estimated cost & duration.

  2. Customized Plan & Itinerary

    Receive a written roadmap with chair-time, recovery schedule, hotel coordination, and visa support if required.

  3. Arrival & Day-1 Review

    Updated scans & clinical exam. Airport pickup & accommodation assistance available on request.

  4. Treatment Sessions

    Appointments are bundled to minimize stay. Evidence-based protocols ensure safety, comfort and predictable results.

  5. Remote Follow-ups

    Virtual reviews via WhatsApp/video. Local referrals arranged in your country if urgent care is required.

⏳ Typical Durations

  • Smile Refresh: 3–4 days
  • Aligners (start): 5–10 days
  • Braces Bonding: 1–2 days + online follow-ups
  • Implant Stage-1: 3–5 days; Stage-2 after ~3 months

🧳 Documents to Carry

  • Passport & visa copy
  • Medical history & current medications
  • Past dental scans or aligner records
  • Travel insurance (recommended)

Frequently asked questions

Can I finish aligner treatment with only two trips?

Often yes. We start with scans & planning, deliver first sets within ~5–10 days, and schedule virtual checks. A second trip finalizes refinements if needed.

Do you help with visa letters?

Yes — we issue appointment confirmation letters with clinic details and itinerary once you share passport info and tentative dates.

How accurate is the currency conversion?

Rates use a public mid-market feed and auto-refresh. Your bank’s settlement rate and fees may differ slightly.

Is post-treatment care available after I return?

Yes, via scheduled tele follow-ups. For urgent care, we coordinate with a local dentist and share your clinical notes.

Ready to plan your trip?

Share your photos, timeline and goals. We’ll propose the safest, fastest path for your smile.

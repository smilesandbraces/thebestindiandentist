FAQs

Good to know
How long do I need to stay in Lucknow?
Most orthodontic starts need 3–5 days initially (records, fitting). Follow-ups can be planned in 4–8 week blocks, with video check-ins in between.
Can I start treatment and continue abroad?
Yes. We coordinate schedules, provide clear instructions, ship retainers if needed, and arrange teleconsults for ongoing guidance.
What about pain and downtime?
Expect mild soreness for 1–3 days after braces activation or new aligners. You can resume sightseeing/work the next day.
Do you help with visas and hotels?
We issue a formal appointment letter for visa and share hotel options in nearby areas.
